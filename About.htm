About Capper News

Capper News provides sports analytics updates and informational SMS alerts to users who choose to subscribe. Our goal is to deliver timely, data-driven insights across major sports leagues including NFL, CFB, NCAAB, NBA, NHL, and MLB.

SMS Notifications & Opt-In Policy

Users may subscribe to receive informational SMS alerts related to sports analytics and account notifications. By opting in, you agree to receive text messages from Capper News. Message frequency varies based on user activity and system alerts.

Opt-In Method: Users opt in via text message by sending a subscription keyword.

Opt-In Confirmation Message:

“You are subscribed to Capper News alerts. Msg frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.”

Help Message:

“Capper News: For support email paulmitri@gmail.com. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.”

Opt-Out Instructions:

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance.

Message and data rates may apply.
